Description of the Recipe

This is a easy to make Lasagna recipe which is made up of Chicken. It is a Italian dish which is done by layering Pasta, meat, and cheese. It is perfect meal for dinner with family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • pasta
  • olive oil
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook ground Chicken until browned; mix in marinara sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish.
  5. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until bubbly and golden.
  7. Let cool for 10 minutes before serving.
