Mutton Curry

Home

Description

Mutton curry is a flavorful, rich, and spicy dish that’s a favorite in Indian and South Asian cuisine. Tender pieces of mutton are slow-cooked in a blend of aromatic spices, tomatoes, and onions for a mouthwatering result.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 cups yogurt
  • 3 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large pan and sauté onions until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes.
  3. Add turmeric, chili powder, and salt; cook for another minute.
  4. Stir in the mutton pieces and cook until browned.
  5. Add tomato puree and yogurt; mix well.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  7. Sprinkle garam masala and cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.
